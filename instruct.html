  1. Visit Google Takeout and login with your Google account.

  2. Deselect all items except for "Location History". Click next step.

  3. Follow the prompts displayed on screen, selecting export once as a .zip file.

  4. Within a few minutes, you should receive an email to download the contents of the takeout.

  5. Download and extract the archive.

  6. Locate the .json file for the month you want to test under /Location History/Semantic Location History/2020.

  7. Upload the relevant file to the web service.
